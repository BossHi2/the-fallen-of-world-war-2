There are merely two World War II leaders alive today: Tenzin Gyatso of Tibet and Simeon II of Bulgaria

Out of the 16.4 million American WW2 veterans, only 45,418 are still alive today.

Out of the 3.5 million Holocaust survivors, only 196,600 are still alive today.

By the year 2043, the last US World War 2 veteran will pass away. As the number of people who lived through World War 2 will reach zero in under two decades, it is crucial that we document and remember the true horror of the war.
