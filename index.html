Out of the 16.4 million American WW2 veterans, only 45,418 are still alive today.

Out of the 3.5 million Holocaust survivors, only 196,600 are still alive today.

The Department of Veterans Affairs estimates that the last US World War 2 veteran will pass away in 2043. As the number of people who lived through World War 2 will reach zero in under two decades, it is crucial that we document and remember the true horror of the war.
